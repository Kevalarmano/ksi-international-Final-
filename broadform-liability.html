Broadform Liability

Broadform Liability insurance is a comprehensive form of public liability coverage designed to protect businesses against a wide range of third-party risks. This policy covers claims arising from personal injury, property damage, and legal costs incurred as a result of the insured’s business activities. What sets Broadform Liability apart is its wider scope—it often includes product liability, advertising liability, and coverage for incidents not typically included in standard liability policies.

It is particularly useful for businesses operating in complex environments, where exposure to multiple forms of risk is significant. Whether you manage a construction site, provide professional services, or manufacture goods, Broadform Liability provides peace of mind by offering strong protection against unforeseen claims. At KSI INTERNATIONAL, we tailor policies to suit your unique risk profile, ensuring you have the right level of cover to operate confidently.
