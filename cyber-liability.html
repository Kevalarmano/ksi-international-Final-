What is Cyber Liability Insurance?

Cyber liability insurance protects your business from the financial consequences of cyberattacks, data breaches, and other online threats. As technology evolves, the risks associated with online business operations grow—making protection more critical than ever. Our policy offers coverage for notification costs, data recovery, legal fees, and more.

Who Needs It?

Any business that stores sensitive data online, operates via e-commerce, or handles client information should consider cyber liability insurance. Whether you're a tech company, law firm, or healthcare provider, cyber threats can strike at any time.

What’s Covered?

  • Data breach response
  • Cyber extortion
  • Business interruption
  • Third-party liability
  • Regulatory penalties
  • Forensics and legal defense
